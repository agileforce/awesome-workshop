Summary With Jumbotron

Lets add the top part of our design that holds the brief summary of Poh's life to the web page.

We will use a container to hold this summary and use the Jumbotron component of Bootstrap within that container.

First lets remove some of the html code we do not need in index.html

<h1>Simple web page</h1>

    <p>
      Hello, my name is <strong>Poh</strong> and I am a Panda who loves Kung Fu.
    </p>

    <img src="images/kungfu-poh.gif"
         alt="Poh practising his Kung Fu moves - animated gif"
         id="kungfu-poh-img" />

Now lets add the container and Jumbotron to the page, along with our image of Poh doing kungfu

Add the following code to the index.html file, on the line after the body tag.

<div class="container">
      <h1 id="website-title">The Story of Poh</h1>
      <div class="jumbotron">
          <div class="pull-right">
            <img src="images/kungfu-poh.gif"
                 id="kungfu-poh-img" >
            </div><!-- .pull-right-->
            <p>
              Hello, my name is <strong>Poh</strong> and I am a Panda who loves Kung Fu.
              I love to watch the Furious Five in action at the Jade palace.
            </p>
            </div><!-- .jumbotron -->
