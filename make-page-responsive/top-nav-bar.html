Top Nav Bar menu

Top Nav Bar

This section is work in progress, sorry

If you have multiple pages on your website or lots of different sections all on one page, you may want to include a menu on your page to help people jump to specific parts of your page or site.

Types of Nav bar

You can have a navbar that just appears at the top of the page and disappears off the top when you scroll the web page.

You can also have a navbar that stays at the top of the web page even when you scroll.
