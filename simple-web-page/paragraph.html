Paragraph

The p tag represents paragraphs in the text content of your web page.

Browsers automatically add some white space (a margin) before and after a paragraph, so its a simple way to group relevant text content together.

<p>This is a single line paragraph.</p>

<p>This is another paragraph that is quite long and contains multiple lines.  As it is so long it will wrap the lines of the text as it displays it.</p>

<p>
  Anything more than a single space   between words is ignored, even after a full stop.
  Tags be put on the same line as the content they define, or for block tags like paragraphs, they can be put on the lines before and after.
